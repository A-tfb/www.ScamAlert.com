About Scam Alert News

At Scam Alert News, our mission is to keep you informed about the latest scams and fraud schemes, helping you stay safe in an increasingly complex digital world.

Founded in 2024, Scam Alert News was created by a group of cybersecurity professionals dedicated to educating the public on how to protect themselves from scammers. We provide up-to-date information on various types of scams, from phishing emails to investment fraud, along with resources to help you recognize and report suspicious activities.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced professionals in cybersecurity, journalism, and consumer protection. We work tirelessly to bring you accurate and timely information to keep you and your loved ones safe.
